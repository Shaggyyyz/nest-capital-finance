Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Nest Capital Finance Pty Ltd (ACN 691 621 191) ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect information necessary to provide you with credit assistance and finance products. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Identity details (Name, DOB, address, driver's license number).
  • Contact details (Phone, email).
  • Financial details (Income, expenses, assets, liabilities, credit history).
  • Employment history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Assess your eligibility for finance products.
  • Submit applications to lenders on your behalf.
  • Comply with legislative requirements (e.g., AML/CTF Laws).
  • Communicate with you regarding your application.

3. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your personal information to:

  • Lenders, mortgage insurers, and valuers.
  • Credit Reporting Bodies (to obtain your credit report).
  • Regulatory bodies (e.g., ASIC) where required by law.
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., IT support, cloud storage) located in Australia or overseas.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. We use secure digital storage and encryption where appropriate.

5. Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. If you believe the information is incorrect, incomplete, or out of date, please contact us to update it.
