Our Services

From your first home to your business expansion, we have the specialist knowledge to guide you.

Home Lending

Whether buying your first home, upgrading, or renovating, we access over 60 lenders to find the right rate and structure for you.

  • First home buyer assistance & government grants
  • Next home buyer solutions & bridging finance
  • Refinancing & loan health checks
  • Construction & renovation loans

Investment Loans

Maximize your returns with interest-only terms, offset accounts, and equity release strategies tailored for investors.

  • Residential investment property finance
  • Portfolio building & multi-property strategies
  • Negative gearing & tax optimization structures
  • Equity release for next investment

Refinancing

Rates change. Ensure you aren't paying "loyalty tax" to your bank. We conduct free health checks to lower your repayments.

  • Free loan health checks & rate comparisons
  • Debt consolidation strategies
  • Switch from interest-only to principal & interest
  • Access better loan features & flexibility

Commercial Finance

Buying your own office, warehouse, or retail space? We navigate commercial lending policies to get you the capital you need.

  • Commercial property purchase & refinance
  • Business equipment & vehicle finance
  • Business expansion & working capital
  • Development & construction finance

SMSF Lending

Take control of your superannuation by purchasing residential or commercial property through your Self Managed Super Fund.

  • Limited recourse borrowing arrangements (LRBA)
  • Residential & commercial property in SMSF
  • SMSF setup & compliance guidance

Asset Finance

Need a new car, truck, or specialized equipment for your business? We provide competitive rates for asset finance.

  • Car loans & novated leasing
  • Commercial vehicle & truck finance
  • Equipment & machinery finance

Our Philosophy

At Nest Capital, we believe that financial freedom requires more than just a loan; it requires an entourage. Our philosophy is simple:

**One team, one briefing.**

We don't treat you like an anonymous bank application. We treat you as a partner. By surrounding you with experts in finance, property advocacy, and wealth strategy, we level the playing field against the banks and the property market.

With access to over 60 lenders and a team that has settled over $700M in loans, we have the scale to get you the best deal and the boutique service to ensure you feel supported every step of the way.
